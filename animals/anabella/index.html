Anabella

Young Highland Cow

Anabella is the youngest member of the Highland cow family, and her playful energy is infectious. With her fluffy coat still developing its full Highland glory, she bounds around the pasture with the enthusiasm only a young calf can muster.

She's endlessly curious, often getting into minor mischief by exploring places she probably shouldn't. Her mother, Lebella, watches over her with patient amusement as Anabella discovers the world one adventure at a time. She has a particular fondness for chasing butterflies and splashing in puddles after rain.

Quick Facts

  • Species: Highland Cow (Calf)
  • Arrived: September 2024 (born on farm)
  • Personality: Playful, curious, energetic
  • Favorite Food: Fresh clover and her mother's milk
