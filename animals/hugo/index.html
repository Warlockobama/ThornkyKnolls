Hugo

Draft Horse

Hugo is a magnificent draft horse with a glossy black coat and feathered feet that make him look like he's wearing boots. His powerful build and strong muscles speak to his heritage as a working horse, though these days he enjoys a more leisurely life at Thorny Knolls.

Despite his imposing size, Hugo is a total softie with a playful spirit. He loves to run through the pasture at full gallop, his mane and tail flowing dramatically behind him. He has an amusing habit of "helping" with farm chores by picking up tools in his mouth and carrying them around, though not necessarily to where they're needed.

Quick Facts

  • Species: Draft Horse
  • Arrived: June 2024
  • Personality: Playful, helpful, energetic
  • Favorite Food: Oats with molasses
