Lebella

Highland Cow

Lebella is the matriarch of the Highland cow herd, with a regal bearing that commands respect from all the farm animals. Her long, elegant horns curve gracefully, and her coat is a rich auburn that catches the sunlight beautifully.

She takes her role as herd leader seriously, always the first to investigate any unusual sounds or newcomers to the farm. Despite her authoritative presence, Lebella has a soft spot for the farm's younger animals and can often be found keeping a watchful eye over them during their afternoon play.

Quick Facts

  • Species: Highland Cow
  • Arrived: March 2024
  • Personality: Protective, regal, nurturing
