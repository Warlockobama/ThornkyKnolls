Raider

Highland Cow

Raider is a robust Highland bull with a reddish-brown coat and an adventurous spirit that earned him his name. He's always the first to explore new areas of the farm and has a talent for finding weak spots in fences - though he never goes far, preferring to simply prove that he could if he wanted to.

Despite his bold personality, Raider is incredibly sweet-natured and has never shown aggression toward people or other animals. He's protective of the herd in his own way, keeping watch while the others graze. His favorite pastime is rolling in the mud puddle near the barn, emerging looking like a creature from a monster movie until someone hoses him off.

Quick Facts

  • Species: Highland Bull
  • Arrived: August 2024
  • Personality: Adventurous, protective, mischievous
  • Favorite Food: Corn stalks and pumpkins in autumn
