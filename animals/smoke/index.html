Smoke

Highland Cow

Smoke is a striking Highland cow with a silver-gray coat that shimmers almost blue in certain light, giving him his distinctive name. His long horns sweep upward elegantly, and his thick, wavy coat makes him look like he's perpetually ready for a photo shoot.

He's the laid-back member of the Highland herd, preferring to spend his days grazing peacefully and avoiding drama. Smoke has a calming presence and often serves as a mediator when the younger animals get into squabbles. He's particularly fond of rainy days, when he can stand outside and let the water run through his magnificent coat.

  • Personality: Calm, peaceful, photogenic
