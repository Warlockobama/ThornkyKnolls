Anabella is the farm's newest addition and embodies all the energy and fearlessness of youth. This Highland calf arrived in early spring and immediately set about exploring every corner of Thorny Knolls, much to Lebella's patient exasperation.

Her fluffy coat is still developing its full Highland thickness, but what she lacks in size, she makes up for in personality. Anabella has discovered that mud puddles are the greatest invention ever and can often be found covered head to hoof in dirt, prancing around like she's won a prize.

She's formed an unlikely friendship with Hugo, the massive draft horse, and the two can often be seen playing chase across the pasture. Anabella charges with all the confidence of a bull, while Hugo pretends to be terrified of the tiny calf.
