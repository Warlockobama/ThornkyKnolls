Argus is a dignified standard donkey with a gray coat and wise, knowing eyes. He's the quiet observer of Thorny Knolls, preferring to watch the farm's daily activities from his favorite spot by the fence line rather than getting involved in the chaos.

Despite his reserved nature, Argus is incredibly loyal and protective of his fellow animals. He has a particular bond with the smaller creatures of the farm and has been known to stand guard when they're feeling vulnerable. His deep, resonant bray can be heard across the farm, usually in response to Dobby's more frequent vocalizations.
