Dobby is a charming miniature donkey with an outsized personality. Standing just over three feet tall, he makes up for his small stature with boundless confidence and a mischievous streak that keeps everyone on their toes.

He's the self-appointed farm alarm system, braying loudly whenever visitors arrive or anything unusual happens. Despite his noisy protests, Dobby is incredibly affectionate and loves nothing more than getting belly rubs and ear scratches. He has a special friendship with Pumpkin, often seen resting in her shadow on hot summer days.
