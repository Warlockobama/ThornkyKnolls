Hugo is a magnificent draft horse with a glossy chestnut coat, flowing mane, and feathered hooves that make him look like he stepped out of a storybook. His size is impressive, but his personality is even bigger—Hugo loves to play and can often be found initiating games with the other farm animals.

Despite being the largest animal on the farm, Hugo is remarkably gentle and aware of his size. He's careful around the smaller animals and has formed an unlikely friendship with Anabella, pretending to be afraid when she charges at him during their evening play sessions.

Hugo also takes his role as a working horse seriously. He helps with farm tasks when needed and seems to genuinely enjoy having a job to do, though he's just as happy spending the afternoon rolling in the grass or trying to steal apples from the orchard.
