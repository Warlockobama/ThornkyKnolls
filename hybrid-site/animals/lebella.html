Lebella is the undisputed matriarch of the Highland herd. With her commanding presence and watchful eye, she keeps the younger cows in line while maintaining a protective watch over the entire farm. Her thick, russet coat and sweeping horns give her a regal appearance that matches her personality perfectly.

She has a special bond with Pumpkin and takes her role as herd leader seriously. Lebella is often seen positioning herself between the herd and anything she perceives as a potential threat—even if that "threat" is just the farmer bringing treats.

Despite her stern demeanor, Lebella has a soft spot for the farm's younger animals, particularly Anabella. She's been known to gently nudge calves back to their mothers and has even stood guard while Dobby takes his afternoon naps.
