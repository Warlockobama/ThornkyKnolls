Pumpkin arrived at Thorny Knolls on a crisp autumn morning, her shaggy orange-brown coat gleaming in the early light. She's a majestic Highland cow with a gentle soul and an insatiable curiosity about everything on the farm.

Despite her impressive horns and sturdy build, Pumpkin is remarkably gentle. She has a habit of approaching visitors slowly, lowering her head for scratches behind her fuzzy ears. Her favorite spot is under the old oak tree, where she spends afternoons watching the world go by with her big, soulful eyes.

Pumpkin has become something of a farm ambassador, always the first to greet newcomers—both human and animal. Her patient, curious nature makes her the perfect introduction to life at Thorny Knolls.
