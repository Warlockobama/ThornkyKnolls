Sega is an imposing mammoth donkey, standing tall with a muscular build and a coat of deep charcoal gray. Despite his impressive size, he's remarkably gentle and moves with surprising grace for such a large animal.

He serves as the gentle giant of Thorny Knolls, often acting as a calming presence when the other animals get too worked up. Sega has a deep, rumbling voice that he uses sparingly, but when he does bray, everyone on the farm takes notice. He's particularly fond of young Anabella and can often be seen letting her play around his massive hooves.
