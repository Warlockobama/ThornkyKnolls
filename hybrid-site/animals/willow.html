Willow is a graceful mule with a sleek brown coat and intelligent, expressive eyes. She's the problem-solver of the farm, often figuring out how to open latches or find creative ways to reach treats that seem out of reach.

Her intelligence is matched only by her stubbornness - when Willow decides she doesn't want to do something, no amount of coaxing will change her mind. However, she's also incredibly gentle and patient, especially with the farm's smaller animals. She has a particular fondness for standing in the shade and swishing her tail rhythmically to keep flies away.
