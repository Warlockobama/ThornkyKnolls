About Thorny Knolls

Welcome to Thorny Knolls, a small farm where Highland cows, donkeys, mules, and horses live together in harmony (most of the time). Each animal brings their own personality, quirks, and stories to the farm.

This site is a collection of profiles and tales from our daily life together. From morning chores to sunset shenanigans, there's never a dull moment when you have a farm full of characters.
