Sunset Trouble Notes

Mischief under the amber sky

Just when the shadows stretch long enough to reach the barn, the herd remembers their second wind. Dobby recruits Hugo for a secret expedition along the fence line, Pumpkin herds Anabella back toward the barn (twice), and Mabel insists on a final brush out even though she was brushed an hour ago.

Raider handles sunset patrol, trotting the perimeter with Smoke matching strides beside him. They pause at the far gate, trade a nod with Willow, and wait until the last hen hops to her roost before giving us the all-clear.

We close the evening with a handful of oats scattered like stardust across the barn aisle. Fireflies ignite over the clover, the frogs tune up down by the creek, and the whole ranch exhales into night while the donkeys whisper plans for tomorrow’s antics.
