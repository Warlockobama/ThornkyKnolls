The Barn Boss

Willow's Ration Audit

Willow has a schedule memorized down to the minute. She lines up the herd two-by-two at feeding time, nudging stragglers into position with a precise hip bump. If the grain scoop rattles three seconds late, she stares directly through the feed room wall until someone moves faster.

Tonight she conducted a surprise inspection of the hay loft. She trotted up the ramp, sniffed every bale, then dragged one down the aisle to redistribute flakes evenly. Argus stood by offering moral support; Dobby pretended to take notes.

When the chores were finished, Willow perched on the tire of the old tractor, ears twitching, until she heard the final click of the paddock gate. Only then did she settle onto the straw pile, satisfied that the night crew could rest easy.

Field Notes

  • Favorite tool: Brass bell tied to the tack hook (she rings it once for “move out”)
  • Best bribe: Slices of orange sweet potato
  • Leadership style: Gentle shove followed by approving hum
  • Quirk: Counts every hoofprint on the barn mat
  • Catchphrase: “Timelines matter.”
