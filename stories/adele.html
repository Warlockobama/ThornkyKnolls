Adele

Fullblood Highland · LaBella × Trillium

Adele is a fullblood Highland heifer calf out of HHH LaBella (Regn. #63503) and sired by AF Nonsuch Trillium (D) (Regn. #63953), known around the barn as Thor. She is a full sibling to Anabella, carrying the same proven cross of dun and brindle Highland genetics.

Her dam, LaBella, is a dun fullblood cow sired by Skye High Cornerstone (D)+, tracing through LEA Haaken (D) and CH Aloysius (D), out of Sweetbrier Naug Bella (D) who descends from Sunset Yardley and Sweetbrier Naugee. Her sire, Trillium, is a brindle fullblood bull bred by Ayrshire Farm, sired by LiTerra Silver Nitro (D) through LiTerra Silverado (ET,PV,D) and Trafalgar Nathan (D), out of AF Nonsuch Wind 25W (D) through TBR Motown and Thorbardin's Gandolf (D).

Adele is coming along nicely on grass, filling out with good bone and a coat that's thickening up well. She has been handled since birth and is building trust with the halter. She shows her dam's calm, steady disposition and her sire's solid build. As a second fullblood heifer from the LaBella × Trillium pairing, she confirms the consistency of this cross.

Where Anabella is all speed and curiosity, Adele is the observer. She watches everything first — how Lebella checks the fence, where Pumpkin stands in the morning, which gate opens at which time — then does it herself like she had it memorized.

She's not timid, just deliberate. She'll walk up to you on her own terms, lean into a scratch, and stay there longer than any of the others. She's the calf you don't have to chase. She already decided you're fine.
