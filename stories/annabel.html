Annabel

Fullblood Highland · LaBella × Trillium

Annabel is a fullblood Highland heifer calf out of HHH LaBella (Regn. #63503) and sired by AF Nonsuch Trillium (D) (Regn. #63953), known around the barn as Thor. She carries genetics from both sides of deep Highland pedigrees raised on grass in Virginia.

Her dam, LaBella, is a dun fullblood cow sired by Skye High Cornerstone (D)+, tracing through LEA Haaken (D) and CH Aloysius (D), out of Sweetbrier Naug Bella (D) who descends from Sunset Yardley and Sweetbrier Naugee. Her sire, Trillium, is a brindle fullblood bull bred by Ayrshire Farm, sired by LiTerra Silver Nitro (D) through LiTerra Silverado (ET,PV,D) and Trafalgar Nathan (D), out of AF Nonsuch Wind 25W (D) through TBR Motown and Thorbardin's Gandolf (D).

Annabel is developing well on grass, showing good bone and a nice coat coming in. She's been handled since birth and is progressing with halter work. She has her dam's balanced frame and her sire's confident carriage. As a fullblood heifer out of a proven LaBella × Trillium cross, she represents solid breeding stock for any Highland program.

Annabel treats every day like a field trip. She picks a new thing to investigate — the rain gauge, a fence post, a bucket someone left out — circles it twenty times, touches it with her nose, then takes off on a lap like she just won something.

She shadows Lebella most of the morning, but by afternoon she's testing boundaries. She'll wear a halter for about four minutes before wriggling loose, then come right back like she wants to try again. She's quick, curious, and not even a little bit shy about it.
