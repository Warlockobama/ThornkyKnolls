Ness of Thorny Knolls

Fullblood Highland · Pumpkin Spice × Trillium

Ness is a fullblood Highland bull calf out of RVF Pumpkin Spice (Regn. #63162) and sired by AF Nonsuch Trillium (D) (Regn. #63953), known around the barn as Thor. He is the first calf from a red × brindle cross that brings together two strong Highland pedigrees.

His dam, Pumpkin Spice, is a red fullblood cow born April 9, 2021, sired by STR Optimus Prime (AI,D) through Twinflower MacDonald (AI) and Star Lake Amber to Star Lake Blackberry. Pumpkin's dam is RVF Truly's Treasure, descending from Sunset Limited Edition (CA,AI,D) and Sunset Eb's Yours Truly through Ebony-Lass of Sunset. She was bred by Matthew and Wendi Campbell.

His sire, Trillium, is a brindle fullblood bull born September 28, 2021 at Ayrshire Farm, sired by LiTerra Silver Nitro (D) through LiTerra Silverado (ET,PV,D) and Trafalgar Nathan (D), out of AF Nonsuch Wind 25W (D) through TBR Motown and Thorbardin's Gandolf (D).

He's growing well on grass and already showing his dam's frame — good width, level topline, and a stance that says he knows where he's standing. He has been handled since birth and is calm for his age. As a fullblood bull calf from a Pumpkin Spice × Trillium cross, he could develop into a solid herd sire prospect or a quality steer depending on how he matures.

Ness showed up with Pumpkin's confidence and hasn't second-guessed anything since. He figured out the hay rack on day one, the water trough on day two, and by day three he was standing at the top of the hill like he owned the view.

Pumpkin keeps him in line without making a fuss about it. One look and he redirects. No drama, no chasing, just a steady little bull calf who already acts like he's read the schedule.
