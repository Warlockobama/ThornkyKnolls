RVF Pumpkin Spice

Registration #63162 · Fullblood Highland

Pumpkin Spice is a red, horned, fullblood Highland cow born April 9, 2021, registered under herd ID RV5K. She was bred by Matthew and Wendi Campbell. Her sire is STR Optimus Prime (AI,D), tracing back through Twinflower MacDonald (AI) and Star Lake Amber to Star Lake Blackberry. Her dam is RVF Truly's Treasure, descending from Sunset Limited Edition (CA,AI,D) on the top side and Sunset Eb's Yours Truly through Ebony-Lass of Sunset on the bottom.

Pumpkin carries a rich, even red coat with a full, heavy fringe and solid conformation throughout. She has good width across the chest, a level topline, and clean horn set with a symmetrical curve. She holds condition well on grass year-round through Virginia's seasons and has proven herself as a reliable producer — her first calf by AF Nonsuch Trillium (D), Ness, is a strong bull calf already showing his dam's frame and temperament.

She is handled daily, halter-trained, and stands quietly for hoof care and veterinary work. She has a calm, steady disposition that passes to her calves, making her an ideal foundation cow for any program focused on temperament and classic Highland type.

Pumpkin is the cow the others check in with. When something new shows up — a tractor parked in a different spot, a visitor at the gate — she's the one who walks over first, sizes it up, and decides whether the rest of the herd should care. Usually her answer is a slow blink and everyone goes back to grazing.

She's got a thing about routine. Same spot at the hay rack, same order through the gate, same shoulder she leans against you with when she wants a scratch. If you skip her morning forehead rub she'll stand two feet away and stare until you remember. Not pushy — just clear about how things work around here.
